# Fymo Marketing Strategy

This document outlines the initial launch and dissemination strategy for Fymo (`projectfymo.com`).

## 1. Brand Identity
The Fymo brand is defined by its **"Blue on Dark Blue"** identity. This is the primary logo that will be used across all social media and internal materials.

### Logo & Brand Assets
- **Primary Logo**: [pfa_website_logo.png](./pfa_website_logo.png) (Blue on Dark Blue text).
- **Secondary Asset**: [logo.svg](./frontend/src/logo.svg) (React-themed backup).

> [!NOTE]
> The user has requested to stick strictly to the existing logo style. No gradients or variations should be used unless explicitly approved.

## 2. Channels & Strategy
### Instagram/TikTok (Management: GF)
- Focus on showing the "Live" state of a financial command center.
- Keywords: *Control*, *Clarity*, *Net Worth*, *Command Center*.
- Content: Reels showing the "Total Assets" and "Net Worth" cards updating in real-time.

### Word of Mouth
- **Personal Outreach**: Friends, family, and acquaintances are the primary initial users.
- **"Demo Mode" Showcase**: Use the public [demo link](https://personal-finance-app-18cbc.web.app/) to show value before requiring a sign-up.

## 3. Core Messaging
- *"Your Personal Net Worth Command Center."*
- *"Don't just track your money. Master it."*
