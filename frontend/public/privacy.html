Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 27, 2026

Financial HQ ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

1. Information Collection

We use Plaid to connect your financial accounts. When you link an account, Plaid provides us with read-only access to your account data, including balances, transactions, investments, and liabilities. We do not see or store your bank login credentials; these are handled securely by Plaid.

2. Use of Information

Your financial data is used solely to provide you with the Net Worth Command Center features, including asset tracking, debt management, and tax estimation. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data:

  • Encryption In-Transit: All data is transmitted via HTTPS (TLS 1.2+).
  • Encryption At-Rest: Your data is stored in Google Firestore, which uses AES-256 encryption.
  • Access Control: Access to your data is strictly limited to your authenticated account via Firebase Security Rules.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated financial data at any time through the application settings.
